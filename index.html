Who We Are – The Core Team of Kritrim Dhi (2024-25)

कृत्रिम धी (Kritrim Dhi) is a student-led AI & ML society of Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta Institute of Professional Studies (ADGIPS), dedicated to fostering innovation, research, and collaboration in the fields of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions.
